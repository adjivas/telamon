A "Path" is essentially Rust's notion of a name.

It's represented as a sequence of identifiers, along with a bunch of supporting information.

E.g. std::cmp::PartialEq

Fields

span: Span segments: Vec<PathSegment>

The segments in the path: the things separated by ::. Global paths begin with keywords::CrateRoot.
